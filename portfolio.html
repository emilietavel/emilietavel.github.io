About Me

"I don’t just create content. I create connection."

Hi, I’m Emilie. I’m 23 years old and I have 3 passions: photography, travel, and sports. I've been creating content since I was 18. What started as pure intuition and a lot of creative energy quickly grew into a loyal community of nearly 3K followers on TikTok with more than 4 million views and 700K likes on my content.

Over the past year, I’ve turned that passion into professional UGC, combining years of experience with my background in tourism. I've had the opportunity to work with The Garden Arequipa and Qema Mancora. So far my collaborations have been focused on the hospitality industry because of my tourism background, but I want to expand into lifestyle and sports content creation as well.
